A bit about me

With a background in print design, graphic design and typography and experience running a successful design agency in Cape Town, an urge to travel found me travelling to London on a 2-year working holiday visa in 1999.  After travelling around Europe, to top up my funds I joined the start-up (at the time) Fish4.  Fish4 was the largest portal at the time for newspapers to list their classified adverts online (jobs, homes, lettings and cars). Here I became a web developer and after 2 years, they sponsored my work visa - I worked there for a further 7 years before I finished due to family commitments. Since then, I have built and run my own luxury brand drop shipping business which I have unfortunately had to close due to the logistic implications of international shipping post Brexit.  

I have worked at King's College London as a web developer and content manager, and my last role was with Christian Aid as aUX/UI Web developer.  This role relied more heavily on my design and webdev experience to plan, design and develop a responsive webportal within the brand guidelines. Planning and designing the UI/UX, considering a rather difficult demographic, was challenging and thoroughly enjoyable.  This project was hugely successful and saw the site being used exactly as it was planned and increased revenue by 400% in May. My enjoyment and satisfaction in using the skills I have learned over my extensive design career led me to want to take a sideways step to more design centred work while still enjoying elements of web development and best practice.

Always inspired and looking for the next best thing in technology, I am in the process of learning to create new community apps for community building e.g. yoga community, subscription services like online design courses, gaming and music to name but a few.  This technology is still new, but Ithink this is going to be a very exciting new industry development.  This is cutting edge and the future of socialmedia.

I believe that I could be an asset and enjoy working under pressure. I enjoy multitasking and am very happy to liaise with different departments and clients. I look forward to hearing back from you.  

 Thanks, and regards
Angela Goodwin
