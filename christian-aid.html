Christian Aid sample pages

This role relied heavily on my design experience to plan, design and develop an easy to implement responsive webportal for Christian Aid fundraising, adhering to stringent brand guidelines and creating new brand assets. The role involved presenting low and high fidelity UX/UI drafts to key stakeholders. I then went on to code the frontend with the development team.

This is a snapshot of the dashboard designs signed off by the Digital Lead and stakeholders. These are high fidelity user experience journeys created in Sketch. For final design signoff all pages in the portal had both desktop and mobile design examples for the development team to use as a framework of look and feel and responsive design elements.
