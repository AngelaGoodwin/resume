Welcome!

Creating meaningful design experiences that work

Hi, I'm Angela,
I'm a designer, a singer, an illustrator and a mum

Design is at the heart of everything I do. I see logo's in the clouds and I have a love of what makes people tick, which is useful when creating user experiences. There is always a song on my lips and a pencil doodling something or other. Love the inspiration of seeing a design come to life or seeing other designers use ideas and colours that blow me away because I haven't looked at things from that angle. I am the eternal student...

Let's work together!

I'd love to work with you no matter how big or small the project or job. I'm available for web, print, digital media, short term projects, one off designs. Drop me an email if you want to chat or collaborate.
